What ClearRoll can't promise

ClearRoll's scanning is genuinely useful, but it isn't perfect, and we won't pretend otherwise.

Document, card, and text detection depends on image quality, angle, lighting, and OCR accuracy — some sensitive items may not be detected, and some non-sensitive items may occasionally be flagged. Sensitive-image detection depends entirely on Apple's own on-device model (Sensitive Content Analysis), which we don't control and can't override.

ClearRoll is a helpful tool for finding things you might have missed — it is not a guarantee that your camera roll contains nothing sensitive after running a scan.

Refunds

ClearRoll Pro is sold and billed entirely through Apple's App Store. Refund requests go through Apple, not ClearRoll directly, since Apple holds the payment relationship and processes all App Store transactions:

  • Apple decides refund requests according to its own policies and your local consumer law — ClearRoll has no ability to issue refunds directly or override Apple's decision.
