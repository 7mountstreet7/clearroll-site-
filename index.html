Nothing leaves your phone

ClearRoll doesn't have a server. There's no account to create, no photo ever uploaded, nothing sent anywhere for analysis. Every scan — and every result — stays on the device in your hand.

Once a scan finds something, you decide what happens to it: keep it, hide it from the app's own view, or delete it from your library. ClearRoll never acts on your photos without you telling it to.

A few honest answers

Does ClearRoll ever upload my photos?

No. There's no server for it to talk to. Scanning, matching, and storing results all happen using your iPhone's own processor.

What does "hide" actually do?

It removes an item from ClearRoll's own review queue without touching your photo library. "Delete" is the only action that removes the photo itself, and it always asks first.

Is this a subscription?

No. ClearRoll Pro is a single one-time purchase that unlocks everything, permanently.
